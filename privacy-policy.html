Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

Summary

doabl does not collect, store on servers, or transmit any personal data. Everything you create inside the extension — tutorials, steps, titles and their state — is saved only in your browser, on your device. We have no servers of our own, we use no analytics tools, and we share no information with third parties.

What data is stored and where

  • The steps you create, tutorial titles, timestamps and the state of each step (done / completed) are saved locally through Chrome's chrome.storage.local API.
  • That information never leaves your device: it isn't synced, isn't uploaded to any server, and we have no access to it.
  • If you uninstall the extension or clear Chrome's browsing data, that information is deleted.

Permissions the extension uses, and why

  • storage — save your steps and tutorials locally on your device.
  • sidePanel — show the doabl panel next to the YouTube video.
  • tabs — read the URL and title of the active YouTube video to associate steps with the right video, and activate or open the video's tab when you tap a step.
  • scripting — inject the capture bridge into YouTube tabs that were already open before the extension started, so capturing works without reloading the page.
  • Access to youtube.com — detect the playing video and read its current time to capture timestamps. Only active on youtube.com.

External connections

doabl's only external connection is loading video thumbnails, fetched directly from YouTube's servers (i.ytimg.com). That request is handled by YouTube/Google under their own privacy policy. doabl sends no other data to any server.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, this page will be updated with a new "last updated" date.
